About TeQuest

Where Code Meets Competition

TeQuest is the flagship annual tech competition hosted by the Department of Computer Science & Software Engineering at Jinnah University for Women — a platform built to challenge, inspire, and celebrate the technological talent of JUW students.

From lightning-fast coding sprints to creative interface design battles, TeQuest covers the full spectrum of computer science disciplines, bringing together the sharpest minds on campus for one electrifying day of competition.

Know the Rules

01

Team Composition

Each team must consist of exactly 2 members. Solo and 3-member entries are not permitted.

02

Registration Limit

Each competition accepts a maximum of 20 teams. Registration closes automatically once the limit is reached. Early registration is strongly advised.

03

Unique Student IDs

Every participant must provide a valid JUW student ID. Duplicate IDs will be rejected by the system. Each student may participate in one competition only.

04

Punctuality

All events are held on May 20, 2026 at JUW campus. Teams must report 15 minutes before their scheduled event. Late arrivals will be disqualified.

05

Eligibility

Participants from Batch 26 of CS & SE department are encouraged to appl.
